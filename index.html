Book Review

What others have said about the book

Practical Wisdom:

"Think Like a Monk" excels in translating ancient monk principles into practical advice for modern living. Shetty’s ability to distill profound wisdom into everyday practices makes this book an invaluable guide for anyone seeking to integrate mindfulness and purpose into their daily routine.

Review by Mario
Modern Relevance:

The relevance of monk teachings in today’s fast-paced world is brilliantly showcased in "Think Like a Monk." Shetty adapts timeless principles to address contemporary challenges, making the wisdom of the monks accessible and applicable to our everyday lives.

Review by Ann
Engaging Storytelling:

Jay Shetty’s engaging storytelling is a highlight of "Think Like a Monk." His use of personal anecdotes and relatable experiences brings the teachings to life, making complex concepts easy to understand and deeply resonant.

Review by Jeran
Actionable Exercises:

One of the standout features of "Think Like a Monk" is its actionable exercises. Shetty provides practical meditations and exercises that are easy to incorporate into daily life, helping readers actively work towards achieving peace and clarity.

Review by Sushila
Clarity and Purpose:

"Think Like a Monk" is a powerful guide for anyone seeking clarity and purpose in their life. Shetty’s insights and advice help readers navigate their inner world, making it easier to find direction and meaning amidst the chaos of modern life.

Review by David
